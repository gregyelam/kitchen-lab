MISO BUTTER STEAK SANDO

list_alt Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak 250G
  • White Miso Paste 2 TBSP
  • Unsalted Butter 50G
  • Shokupan (Milk Bread) 2 SLICES
  • Togarashi PINCH
  • Kewpie Mayo TO SERVE

The Method

TIME 15 MINS
LEVEL EASY AF
01

Pat the steak dry and season heavily with salt. Sear in a screaming hot cast-iron pan for 2 minutes per side until a deep mahogany crust forms.

02

Lower the heat and drop in the miso and butter. Baste the steak continuously for 1 minute as the butter foams and smells nutty.

03

Toast your shokupan in the residual steak fat. Slather with mayo, add the sliced steak, and pour over every last drop of that miso butter.
